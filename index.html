About West Cook Water Polo

Our Mission

To offer the highest quality water polo experience possible to the West Cook County area. We believe in the power of water polo to build character, teamwork, and leadership skills in young athletes and seek out to grow the presence of the sport in the region and in Illinois as a whole

What We Offer

  • High School Program: Serving ages 14-18
  • Expert Coaching: Former collegiate players and current high school coaches
  • Competition: Participating in local and regional competitions

Summer High School (18U)
